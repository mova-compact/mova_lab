# About

My name is Serhii Miasoiedov. I work at the intersection of business processes, artificial intelligence, contract-based design, and practical automation.

My focus is not to “add AI to a business”, but to understand what specific work a system should perform, where the boundaries of responsibility lie, what actions are allowed, what must be verified, and what result can be considered correct.

## Where This Comes From

Before working with AI, I spent many years in media: filming, editing, observing people, events, systems, and the way information turns into action.

That experience gave me an important foundation: to see not only the tool, but the process around it. Who makes decisions. Where chaos appears. What repeats. Where a person gets tired. Where structure is needed.

When modern AI tools appeared, one thing became clear: the main problem is not that the model “cannot do something”. The problem is that businesses often do not know how to embed it safely and usefully into a real process.

## My Approach

I look at an AI system as a working loop:

**intent → rules → action → result → verification**

If this chain is missing, the system quickly turns into a chaotic assistant that may sound convincing but has no clear boundaries.

That is why I use a contract-based approach.

A contract describes what the system can do, with what data, through which tools, under what conditions, where human judgment is required, and how the result is verified.

## What I Build

I design and build:

- contract-based AI systems for business processes;
- specialized assistants for specific roles;
- automations for regular reporting and recurring routine;
- systems for controlling AI actions;
- safe workflows for client data;
- practical prototypes for testing a business idea.

## What Matters to Me

I do not believe in automation for the sake of automation.

A strong system should be as simple as possible and as controlled as necessary.

If a normal process is enough, there is no need to build a complex AI system.

If AI works with data, clients, money, or external services, it needs boundaries, verification, and an action log.

## MOVA Lab

MOVA Lab is the place where I bring this approach into a public form: texts, case studies, products, experiments, and practical solutions.

This is not a showroom for “magic AI”.

It is a lab for controlled AI systems that are meant to work in real business: calmly, clearly, and with a verifiable result.
