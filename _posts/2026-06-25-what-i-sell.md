# What I Sell

I do not sell generic AI hype.

I sell a practical path for businesses that want AI to do real work without losing control.

## The three layers

### 1. Consulting

I help businesses find where AI fits into their real processes.

That includes:

- process discovery
- role and boundary mapping
- use-case selection
- implementation design
- risk review

### 2. Contract AI products

I turn the useful part of an AI workflow into a product.

That means a system with:

- clear authority boundaries
- defined inputs and outputs
- controlled execution
- evidence or audit trail
- repeatable delivery

### 3. Specialized Hermes executors

Hermes becomes the delivery surface for a focused job.

With the right skills and tools, it can act as:

- a contract agent
- a marketing assistant
- a business admin assistant
- a report runner
- a workflow executor

## Why this matters

Most AI projects start from tools.

Mine start from the work.

That difference is what makes the result useful, repeatable, and sellable.
